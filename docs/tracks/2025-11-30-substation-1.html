Notes

I built this patch to learn how the Slime Child Audio Substation modules work. I tried to use every module and every feature, including the polyrhythmic sequencer, the sub-oscillators, and the dual-envelope filter -- which gives an interesting push/pull texture to the sound. I used the VCV Random module alongside a Seq-3 sequencer to bounce the CV up and down a few octaves non-deterministically to keep things interesting.
