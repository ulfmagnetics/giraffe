Notes

This was the first patch I put together in VCV Rack Pro in 2025. I wanted to build from the ground up something that could produce interesting sounds that weren't too repetitive. I grabbed the Quantify quantizer from the VCV Library and used Random module to add some RNG into the composition.

There are some weird hacks in here like the Compare modules that trigger the snare drums based on the outputs of a Seq-3 sequencer. There are much easier ways to do this probably but I was trying to focus mainly on core VCV modules in this patch to put a ceiling on how much I needed to learn all at once.
